India is the seventh-largest country by area and the most populous country in the world with over 1.4 billion people. The capital city is New Delhi. India gained independence from British rule on August 15, 1947. The national animal is the Bengal tiger, and the national bird is the peacock. The currency is the Indian Rupee. Hindi and English are the official languages. The Ganges River is the longest river in India, flowing over 2525 kilometers.
