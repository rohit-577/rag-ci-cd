Mangoes are tropical fruits that belong to the genus Mangifera. India is the largest producer of mangoes in the world, producing over 20 million tons annually. There are hundreds of varieties including Alphonso, Kesar, and Hapus. Mangoes are rich in vitamin C, vitamin A, and fiber. The mango is the national fruit of India. A single mango tree can produce fruit for over 40 years. Mangoes were first cultivated in South Asia over 4000 years ago.
