Apples are one of the most widely cultivated fruits in the world. There are over 7500 varieties of apples grown globally. China produces the most apples, followed by the United States and Turkey. Apples are rich in fiber and vitamin C. The old saying 'an apple a day keeps the doctor away' dates back to 1913. Apple trees take 4 to 5 years to produce their first fruit. A medium apple contains about 95 calories. Apples float in water because they are 25 percent air.
