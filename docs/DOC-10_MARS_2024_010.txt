Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second smallest planet in our solar system. It is often called the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface. Mars has two moons named Phobos and Deimos. A day on Mars is 24.6 hours, almost the same as Earth. Mars has the tallest mountain in the solar system, Olympus Mons, which is 21.9 kilometers high. NASA's Perseverance rover landed on Mars in February 2021 and is searching for signs of ancient life.
