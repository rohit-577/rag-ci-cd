Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the most populous metropolitan area in the world with over 37 million residents. The city was originally called Edo until it was renamed in 1868. Tokyo hosted the Summer Olympics in 1964 and 2021. The Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world at 634 meters. The city has 23 special wards and is known for its efficient public transportation system serving over 8 billion passengers annually.
